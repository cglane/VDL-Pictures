VDL Associates, LLC is a design firm specializing in Residential and Commercial Architecture, including Renovations and Historic Restoration. Established in 1996 in the heart of Historic Charleston, the firm’s projects can be found all over the Coastal Carolina region as well as throughout the Southeast.

The firm has also been a recipient of the following prestigious awards:
The Historic Charleston Foundation's Whitelaw Founders Award - 96 Broad Street
Preservation Society of Charleston's Carolopolis Award - 96 Broad Street
Preservation Society of Charleston's Carolopolis Award - 56 Church Street
SC Chapter of the American Institute of Architects' Merit Award - 96 Broad Street
SC Chapter of the American Institute of Architects' Merit Award - 46 South Battery
SC Chapter of the American Institute of Architects' Merit Award - 22 Lamboll Street
